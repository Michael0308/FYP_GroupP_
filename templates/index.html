Performance metrics

We have implemented 3 performance metrics, to compare the performances of our four optimization algorithms

  • Hypervolume:measuring the volume of the objective space dominated by an approximation set and is bounded by a reference point. Larger is better!
  • Inverted Generational Distance: calculating minimum Euclidean distance between the approximation set and the Pareto optimal front. Lower is better!
  • Spacing: measuring how evenly the solutions are distributed in the objective space, focusing on diversity. Lower is better!
